2012 Annual Fund Drive!

This year, November through December, we are asking our supporters to help us promote literacy among African-American children by making a donation to Brown Baby Reads.

In April 2009, Brown Baby Reads launched the website as a resource to parents and teachers.  Within one year, the website experienced rapid growth to over 3,000 hits per month and has continued that growth beyond 5,000 hits per month.  Each month parents and teachers receive monthly book selection titles through our online book club.

Today, over 1,100 parents and teachers have registered and more follow us via Facebook.

Brown Baby Reads has been able to make an impact thanks to the thousands of volunteer hours, in-kind donated services and donations.  Special thanks to generous financial contributions by foundations, businesses, and individuals like you.

In order to sustain and expand our programs, we need your help!  Your donation will help to purchase books for elementary school classroom libraries, further develop our literacy website for children, fund book donations directly to children and parents to help them develop their own home libraries, and expand our programs into new schools and communities.

Please join us by making a donation to Brown Baby Reads.

If you have any questions regarding the Annual Fund Drive, please feel free to contact us by e-mail at info@brownbabyreads.com. Thank you for your support!

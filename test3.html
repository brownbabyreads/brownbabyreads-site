The Mission of Brown Baby Reads is to Celebrate, Encourage, and Promote Literacy among African-American Children

         
 
       

Thank you for visiting our website. We are committed to celebrating and encouraging literacy in African-American children. "What you focus on grows". So, we have chosen to focus on the strength and tenacity of our culture and our children. Our organization celebrates the intellects of our African-American children through their reading.

We are encouraging all parents and caregivers to nurture and develop children's lives through reading books that feature African-American children. We are a myriad of hues, backgrounds, regional descents, experiences, and economic realities. The books we recommend will celebrate that mosaic.

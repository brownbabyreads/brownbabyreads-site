Brownbabyreads.com

The Brown Baby Reads website is designed to provide information and resources for parents, teachers and other stakeholders for African-American children. The website focuses on literacy, reading, and African-American children’s books. Some of the main resources on the site include:

*The African-American Children's Book Database
*The Interactive Black History Timeline for Kids
*Articles, Books, and Academic Research about Literacy and African-American Children
*Newly Released Books & Upcoming Releases

Cocoakidsread.com

The Cocoa Kids Read website is a free culturally-relevant educational resource for elementary-age children ages 7 to 12. The site provides opportunities for children to engage in activities that are designed to build literacy and affirm esteem concurrently.

Click here to visit the cocoakidsread.com website
  

Our classroom support program partners with local elementary school teachers to provide culturally-specific books and resources to classroom libraries. 

For more information on the classroom support program, contact Philinda at philinda@brownbabyreads.com

 

We offer discounted book bundles to our registered website members and our partners. Through our online store, we are attempting to improve access to quality high-interest children's books that reflect the lives and experiences of African-American children.  Our goal is to get culturally relevant books into children's hands, period.

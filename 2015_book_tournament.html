The 2015 Cocoa Kids Read Book Tournament

We are so excited to announce that we will be hosting the Cocoa Kids Read Book Tournament this year. The tournament is a great way to celebrate Black History Month.

Beginning in the first week of February, popular and not-so-popular books with African-American main characters will go head-to-head until a 2015 Cocoa Kids Read champion is crowned. Some books are considered classics, some are newer on the scene, but they're all from the realm of fiction.

There are 3 divisions based on grade: The Picture Book Division for grades K to 1st. The Chapter Book Division I for 2nd and 3rd grades. The Chapter Book Division II for 4th and 5th grades. The brackets in the Picture Book Division contain only 16 books, while both of the Chapter Book Divisions contain 32 books.

Each section of the bracket starts with sixteen books. First round voting for starts February 1st, but the booklists and brackets are available now.

If you’d like to help spread the word, use the #cocoabooktourney hashtag on Twitter or steal the graphic from above!

If you have any questions regarding the Book Tournament, please feel free to contact us by e-mail at tournament@brownbabyreads.com. Thank you for your participation

WELCOME TEACHERS & EDUCATORS!

We recognize that you make a difference in so many childrens' lives, and wenwant to provide you with resources that will assist you in helping your students.

We know how important it is for teachers to have quality curriculum resources in order to free up more time to focus on the other aspects of teaching.

Membership is Free.

Sign up and receive access to all the resources we offer.

We have a Database full of book titles that feature African-American children as main characters. There are over 2,000 books in the database, and the listings grows daily. There are multiple search features that allow you to find authors, topics, reading level, genre, grade, etc.

We also provide a Collection of Teacher's Guides, Lesson Plans, and Discussion Question links for many of the books in the database.

The Research Section provides information on current and pertinent research surrounding African-American children and literacy. The section contains book titles, newpaper articles, and full academic dissertations.

Book Bundles: We reviewed hundreds of books and selected high-interest books to bundle together for purchase at reasonable costs for registered teachers.

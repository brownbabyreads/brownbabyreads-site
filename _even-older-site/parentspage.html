We recognize that you are the most important people in your child's life. We want to empower you with literacy resources and information in order to help your child grow in their literacy and love for reading.

Register now (membership is free) and have access to all our resources for parents:

The Book Database

The database contains over 2,000 books focusing on African-American children. You can search our book database and find books for your children. You can search by: Title, Author, Illustrator, Age Group, Gender, and/or topic.

Parent's Books & Resources

We have recommended books that pertain to improving literacy of our children. Also, our advisory board members contribute articles that relate to literacy.

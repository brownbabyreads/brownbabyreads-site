How You Can Help

 

Get the Word Out
Tell parents and teachers that you know of the free resources available.  Our website www.brownbabyreads.com provides a database with over 1,200 African-American books for children and youth. It has multiple search features by author, title, age group, subject, or keyword.  The website also provides a host of free resource links. 

Financially support our efforts
A donation of any amount is welcome.  We are a 501(3)(c) organization, so all donations are TAX-DEDUCTIBLE. You can give to the general fund or towards a specific project.  100% of monies donated towards individual projects is given directly to the project. http://www.brownbabyreads.com/makeadonation.html (online payments accepted through paypal)

Buy books through the Bookstore
When you purchase books through our bookstore or the book links for Amazon or Ebay, a portion of the purchase goes back to Brown Baby Reads.  If you purchase anything on amazon.com after you enter through our website, Amazon provides a commission to Brown Baby Reads on the Purchase.
